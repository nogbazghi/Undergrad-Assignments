Chaos abounds between ancient Palestine and Rome in this
historic look at early Christianity .
Out of desperation
due to oppresive Roman rule , Jewish Palestine revolts in
a variety of unique ways .
The proliferation of bandits
is only the first indication of this troubled time .
A host
of diviners and self-made holy leaders gain following in
Palestine as people seek a way out of the horrific situation .
This book gives a stunning look at the incredible measures and actions a group will take when their way of life is being destroyed .
